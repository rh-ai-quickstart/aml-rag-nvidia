# Parasol Financial AML Case Notes Template

Parasol Financial

- Case ID:

- Customer Name:

- Customer Type:

- Customer Risk Rating:

- Accounts/Products Involved:

- Alert Type:

- Review Period:

- Assigned Investigator:

- Date Opened:

- Date Closed or Escalated:

## 1. Alert Summary

State what triggered the review and summarize the activity in one short paragraph.

## 2. Customer Context

Summarize relevant onboarding information, account purpose, business description if applicable, ownership profile, expected activity, prior alerts, and any notable risk history.

## 3. Activity Reviewed

Describe the transactions, channels, time period, counterparties, and geographic pattern reviewed. Note whether activity was unusual compared with historical behavior.

## 4. Evidence Considered

List the information sources reviewed, such as account history, customer documentation, prior cases, internal policy guidance, and approved screening results.

## 5. Red Flags Observed

Identify specific red flags from the Suspicious Activity Red Flags Handbook, if any.

## 6. Explanation Considered

Summarize any available explanation from the customer relationship record, supporting documents, or known business context. State whether the explanation was complete, partial, or not available.

## 7. Investigator Assessment

Provide a concise assessment of whether the activity appears reasonably explained or remains materially suspicious. Avoid speculation and tie conclusions to evidence.

## 8. Disposition

Mark one: Closed as explained; Closed as not escalated; Escalated for senior review; Escalated to sanctions; Escalated to fraud; Referred for SAR consideration.

## 9. Rationale

State why the disposition was selected.

## 10. Follow-Up Actions

List any required next steps, including enhanced due diligence updates, account review, documentation requests, or committee referral.

## Note Quality Standard

Notes must be complete enough for QA, management, or audit review without requiring the reader to reconstruct the case from raw system data.
